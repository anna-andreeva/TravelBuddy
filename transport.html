Rail

The cheapest and perhaps the most adventerous way to travel around Europe is by train. Train travel is unusually cheap in Europe.

Plane

For those who prefer more luxurious mode of travel, we recommend flying. Flights within Europe are often frequent and quite cheap, but you also have to consider the cost of airport transfer.

Cars

If you don't want to be dependent on public transport and fancy an attempt at driving on the 'wrong' side of the road, this option is for you. At the wheel of your car, you will be fully in control of your route, which has many benefits.
